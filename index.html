The "Mariachi Nuevo Jalisco", located in San Antonio and Austin, Texas is one of the best mariachi bands that there is to interpret the entire Mexican folklore music, from the joy of one happy rhytm, huapango, rancheras and romantic Mexican feeling. Being a relatively young band, each member has experience of many years of musical career since 1991, played with great artists such as Alejandro Fernandez, Azucena, Pablo Montero, Marco Antonio Solis, Verónica Castro, Lucha Villa, Los Temerarios, Humberto Estrada, Martin Urieta and Cuco Sanchez. with all the joy and a real intense Mexican feeling, Mariachi Nuevo Jalisco invites you to check out your next event.

Originally from San Luis Potosi, Mexico, we have a great repertoire to liven up your event. Don't miss out on listening to us!   Contact.
